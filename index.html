Health Data Science & Artificial Intelligence @ Manchester

Using data-driven research to improve the health and wellbeing of our region and the nation.

What we do

We develop and apply Health Data Science and Artificial Intelligence to a broad range of biomedical and health areas from understanding the causes of human disease at the molecular and cellular level using computational biology and bioinformatics to the study of the health of populations and the development of clinical decision support systems to provide data-driven insight.
